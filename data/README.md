# Datasets

## CUAD (Contract Understanding Atticus Dataset)

Download from the official repository:
- **Source**: https://github.com/TheAtticusProject/cuad
- **File needed**: `cuad-v1-annotated.json`
- **Place in**: `data/cuad/cuad-v1-annotated.json`

CUAD comprises 510 commercial legal contracts with 13,101 expert annotations across 41 clause types.

**Reference**: Hendrycks, D., Burns, C., Chen, A., Ball, S. (2021). CUAD: An Expert-Annotated NLP Dataset for Legal Contract Review. NeurIPS.

## DI2WIN Dataset

The DI2WIN dataset contains Brazilian Portuguese corporate formation contracts annotated with 143 entity types. It was provided by Di2win under a research collaboration agreement.

- **Availability**: Will be released under CC-BY-NC 4.0 license upon publication
- **Contact**: jbm@ecomp.poli.br for access requests
- **Place in**: `data/di2win/`

DI2WIN comprises 359 contracts (312 training, 47 test) with 34,674 annotated entities.
